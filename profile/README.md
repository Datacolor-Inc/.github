Headquartered in Texas, Datacolor.ai focuses on enabling end-to-end digital transformation through AI, data governance, and intelligent automation.

## 🚀 Our Mission

To transform how enterprises build, adopt, and scale trusted AI — combining intelligent agents, governed data workflows, and actionable outcomes in production-ready ecosystems.

Enable seamless data discovery and trust with governed metadata and catalogs.

### 2. **Ingestion**

### 4. **Agentic Activation**

### 5. **Business Process Integration**
Embed AI and governance directly into operational workflows for real-time action and traceability.
